Cities: The Jungle of Man

Metal buildings many times taller going way above our head, providing life to us humans as trees would provide oxygen. The city life is busy, crowded, ugly and beautiful all at the same time. To find the busiest place on earth, you just need to find that part of land that glows in the night, bustling with people and cars seemingly 24/7. This site features the many spectacles of what cities have become in the 21st century.""
